The Kalchakra (Timeless Orbit) is an infinite cycle of time, visualized as a rotating wheel with two distinct halves. It has been rotating since time immemorial and will continue for an infinite future.

AVASARPINI

The Descending Era

Like a wheel moving downwards, human strength, physical height, and happiness gradually decrease.

10 Croda-Crodi Sagaro-pam

UTSARPINI

The Ascending Era

Like a wheel moving upwards, human strength, physical height, and happiness gradually increase!

10 Croda-Crodi Sagaro-pam
